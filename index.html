Man Behind Innovation

DR ZVI PEARLSTEIN
DOCTOR, FATHER, SPEAKER, AUTHOR, INVENTOR, AND TRUMPETER.

Dr Zvi was impacted in the womb and through the course of his life by his mother's emotional pain resulting from her traumatic experiences as a teenager in Poland during the Holocaust. From a young age he had a deep-seated need and calling to find life's meaning and to understand and solve pain. His mother Celia passed in 2019 leaving behind writings and poetry of her experiences.

He received his BA in Biochemistry from Princeton University in 1976 and his MD from New York University School of Medicine in 1981. He completed a 5-year residency in Orthopedic Surgery at SUNY Downstate in Brooklyn in 1986.

He was a Major in the United States Air Force and spent three years practicing orthopedic surgery at Clark Air Base in the Philippines. He then spent one year at Andrews Air Force Base in Wash. DC where he was an assistant professor at the Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences in Bethesda, MD. He did active duty service during Persian Gulf War I. While serving in the Air Force he was a marksman shooter and was awarded the Sikorsky Helicopter Rescue Award.

Dr Zvi is an out-of-the-box visionary thinker and has developed a collection of resources to help optimize health, wellness, and fitness. This “Pain Toolbox” is offered by his company Halbeshekel, Inc., dba Missing Links Health, Inc. and includes:

  • SitFit Chair – total body gym packaged as a portable chair
  • 1 Minute Stretch e-book and videos – a medically essential head to toe muscle and joint full range of motion routine
  • Stretching for Pain Relief – a forty video muscle and joint educational package
  • U R Not What U Eat – A Doctor’s Guide to Transform Your Pain to Wisdom & Joy – book available at Amazon
  • MRI – Med – Surg Solutions – offering consulting on flaws and fixes in pain management and in the development and utilization of MRI for surgical determination
